## Risk Assessment

### Technical Risks

**Memory Scaling**
- Risk: Memory grows too large, performance degrades
- Mitigation: Lifecycle management, compression, pruning

**Pattern Evolution**
- Risk: Patterns evolve incorrectly, system degrades
- Mitigation: Safety layer, validation, rollback mechanisms

**Meta-Learning**
- Risk: System optimizes incorrectly
- Mitigation: Controlled experiments, human oversight

### Market Risks

**Competition**
- Risk: Larger companies build similar systems
- Mitigation: First-mover advantage, open-source strategy, partnerships

**Adoption**
- Risk: Slow adoption by AI labs
- Mitigation: Developer-first approach, research partnerships, open-source

### Execution Risks

**Team Scaling**
- Risk: Can't hire fast enough
- Mitigation: Early hiring, strong culture, competitive compensation

**Technical Complexity**
- Risk: System too complex to build
- Mitigation: Phased approach, research partnerships, AI co-authorship

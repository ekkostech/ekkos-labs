## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style

- **Clean, modern** — Minimal design, focus on content
- **Data-driven** — Charts, graphs, metrics prominently displayed
- **User-focused** — Before/after comparisons, user testimonials
- **Technical credibility** — Architecture diagrams, technical details

### Color Scheme

- **Primary:** ekkOS brand colors
- **Accent:** Green for improvements, red for problems
- **Neutral:** Gray for comparisons, white for clarity

### Typography

- **Headings:** Bold, large, clear hierarchy
- **Body:** Readable, not too small
- **Metrics:** Large, prominent numbers
- **Code/Technical:** Monospace font
